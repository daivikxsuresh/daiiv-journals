About Me

The Story So Far

Good Morning, Good Evening, and Good Night —

I started in supply chain the hands-on way: on warehouse floors at Amazon, in freight brokerage negotiating carrier rates, and managing $100M+ in procurement across five states. Along the way I kept noticing the same thing — the biggest wins never came from working harder, they came from seeing the data differently.

That's what pulled me toward AI. Today I'm pursuing my MS in Business Analytics & AI at NYU Stern, working at the intersection of procurement and machine intelligence at Humana, and publishing monthly deep-dives on supply chains, freight markets, and global economics — read by 25,000+ people. The journals are where the operator's instinct meets the analyst's toolkit.
